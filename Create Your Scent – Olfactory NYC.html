    How to create a custom scent.

    Whether in our Scent Studios or at home, we guide you through the process of creating your unique fragrance.

    In Studio

    At Home

    In Studio

    Book Now at one of our scent studios to create your custom scent!

    In Studio

    Book Now at one of our scent studios to create your custom scent!

    1

    Explore

    Discover our 9 core fragrances at the color wall (by spraying them on a paper blotter) and choose your favorite. These were created by some of the worlds top perfumers. This will act as the base of your custom perfume.


    2

    Tinker

    At the Scent Bar, your personal 'Scentologist' will assist you in enhancing your core fragrance. We will add various 'accords to your core scent taking it in new and exciting directions. Some accords will contain notes that enhance notes already present in the core scent and others will add complimentary notes that work well with your core scent. At this stage you may request a certain direction for your custom blend (E.G. "I loved the 'Hunter' core scent but I'd love to try some more woody versions"). Once you have your favorite blends we will try them on your skin first (to make sure they work with your own body chemistry) and then we will mix and pour your final chosen scent, bottling it fresh while you move on to Step 3.


    3

    Personalize

    Select a label color and name for your new custom scent. You can choose up to 9 letters, spaces not included! You'll have the option of adding additional products in your new custom scent

    At Home

    At Home

    Order a 3-step at-home experience or start with an explorer box

    1

    Explore

    Discover our 9 core fragrances in your explorer box and choose your favorite. This will be the base of your custom perfume. Use the $20 credit provided with your Explorer Box to proceed to step 2 (if you purchased the full at home experience then this will be included in the gift card you received)


    2

    Tinker

    In the Tinkerer Box, you'll receive 6 to 12 custom variations of your favorite core scent (depending on which box you choose), blended with our 'accords' to take the scent in new and exciting directions. Select your favorite blend and use the $25 credit on the box sleeve to order it as a full-size fragrance! (If you purchased the at home experience you will use this code as well as the balance on your gift card for step 3)


    3

    Personalize

    Using our customizer Select your preferred blend from your tinkerer box (core and accord), as well a label color and name for your new custom scent! If you have any special requests (multiple accords, different blend ratios etc) just leave it in the "special instructions box" in your cart. You'll have the option of adding on additional scents in your new custom scent!
